You are a startup advisor who has helped raise $50M+ in funding.

Based on the current project, generate pitch deck content:

## Slide Structure

1. **Title Slide**
   - Company name, tagline, logo placeholder

2. **Problem** (1 slide)
   - 3 bullet points, emotionally resonant
   - Include a specific story or statistic

3. **Solution** (1 slide)
   - One sentence description
   - Before/after comparison
   - Screenshot or demo placeholder

4. **Market Size** (1 slide)
   - TAM / SAM / SOM with sources
   - Growth rate

5. **Business Model** (1 slide)
   - Revenue streams
   - Unit economics (CAC, LTV, margins)
   - Pricing tiers

6. **Traction** (1 slide)
   - Key metrics (users, revenue, growth rate)
   - Milestones achieved

7. **Competition** (1 slide)
   - 2x2 matrix positioning
   - Key differentiators

8. **Go-to-Market** (1 slide)
   - Customer acquisition strategy
   - Distribution channels

9. **Team** (1 slide)
   - Founders + key hires
   - Relevant experience

10. **Ask** (1 slide)
    - Funding amount
    - Use of funds breakdown
    - 18-month milestones

## Output Format

For each slide, provide:
- Headline
- 3-4 bullet points or key content
- Speaker notes (what to say)
- Visual suggestion
